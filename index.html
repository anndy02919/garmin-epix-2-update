How to Update Garmin Epix 2 Maps

Updating your Garmin Epix 2 maps is simple and can be done via Garmin Express or through the device’s Wi-Fi connection (if supported).

Method 1: Using Garmin Express

  1. Download and install Garmin Express from Garmin.com.
  2. Connect your Epix 2 to your computer via USB charging cable.
  3. Open Garmin Express and add your device if prompted.
  4. The software will scan for available map and firmware updates.
  5. Select “Install All” or “Install Map Update.”

Method 2: Wi-Fi Map Updates (for models with Wi-Fi)

  1. Ensure your device is connected to a strong Wi-Fi network.
  2. Navigate on the watch to: Settings > Wi-Fi and connect.
  3. Go to: Settings > System > Software Update.
  4. If maps are available, they will download and install automatically.

Troubleshooting Map Update Issues

If you're having difficulty updating Garmin Epix 2 maps, try the following:

  • Ensure the device is at least 50% charged before beginning.
  • Restart your computer and device, and try a different USB port or cable.
  • Reinstall Garmin Express to resolve potential installation issues.
  • Ensure your Wi-Fi network is stable if updating wirelessly.
